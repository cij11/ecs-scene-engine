A ticket is ready for development when:

## All tickets

- All scrum ticket fields are filled out
- Status is `readyForDev`
- Acceptance criteria are specific and verifiable
- Testing scenarios describe concrete inputs and expected outputs
- Size is estimated

## Tickets with visual output

- Acceptance criteria describe what should be visible (not just "it renders")
- Expected camera position / scene composition is described or referenced
- Success criteria include what a stakeholder would see when reviewing the demo
